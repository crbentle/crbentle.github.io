What we do

Mama Maisha is a Safe Motherhood Intervention Project founded on the belief that the death of a mother is not only a health issue but also a matter of social injustice.

Our multidisciplinary team made up of medical staff (physicians, nurses, community health workers) and educators (community organizers, social workers, volunteers) works to decrease the number of women who die due to complications of pregnancy and childbirth in rural Tanzania. We are committed to ensuring that every mother accesses essential reproductive health services.

Our vision is that all women would have the information, power and tools to ensure wise planning of their families, healthy pregnancy and safe delivery for every child.

Our objective is to reduce maternal mortality in rural Tanzania by increasing knowledge of and access to reproductive health services and contraception and by providing empowerment and accountability for each pregnant woman to make choices for her own health and safety during all stages of childbearing.
